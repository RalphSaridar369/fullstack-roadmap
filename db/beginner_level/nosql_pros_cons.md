## ‚úÖ Pros
- **Flexible schemas** (easy to adapt to new data)  
- **Horizontally scalable** (handle huge datasets)

## ‚ùå Cons
- **Less standardized** than SQL (query languages vary)

- Data integrity must often be handled at the application level  
- Can be **less mature** for complex analytics

## üßê Why NoSQL?
Choose NoSQL when you need **scalable, flexible, or high-performance storage**.

- **Key‚ÄìValue:** Twitter, Instagram, Shopify
